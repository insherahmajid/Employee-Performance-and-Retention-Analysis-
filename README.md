## Employee Performance and Retention Analysis
# Project Overview

This project analyzes employee performance and retention using Python for data exploration and Power BI for interactive dashboard visualization.
The goal is to identify key factors affecting employee performance, training effectiveness, recruitment channels, and retention patterns. The dashboard provides insights that can help HR teams improve workforce planning and employee engagement strategies.
#  Objectives
The project aims to answer the following questions:
-What is the overall employee performance level in the organization?
-Which departments achieve the highest KPI performance?
-How do training scores and previous ratings influence KPI achievement?
-What are the major recruitment channels for hiring employees?
-What is the distribution of employees by age and tenure?
-Do awards and recognition relate to employee performance?
-Which tenure groups contribute the most to performance and productivity?

# Page 2: Performance Analysis
This section explores factors affecting employee performance.
-Visualizations
-Training Score vs KPI Achievement

-Key Findings
-Employees with higher previous ratings tend to achieve KPIs more frequently.
-R&D and Operations departments show the highest KPI achievement rates.

#  Page 3: Employee Retention Analysis
This section focuses on employee tenure, demographics, and recognition.
Visualizations
-Employee Distribution by Length of Service
-Employee Age Distribution
-Performance & Awards by Tenure
-Average Length of Service by KPI Status
# Key Findings
-Most employees stay between 3–7 years, indicating mid-career workforce concentration.
-The 30–40 age group dominates the workforce.
-Employees with 3–10 years tenure contribute the highest KPI performance and awards.
-Tenure alone does not guarantee higher performance, as KPI achievers have slightly lower average tenure.

#  Key Insights

1️⃣ Mid-tenure employees (3–10 years) are the most productive and receive the highest number of awards.

2️⃣ Previous performance ratings strongly influence KPI achievement.

3️⃣ Recruitment channels are heavily dependent on external sourcing, while referral hiring is underutilized.

4️⃣ The workforce is dominated by employees aged 30–40, indicating a skilled mid-career talent pool.

#  Business Recommendations

-Strengthen employee recognition programs, as award-winning employees tend to show stronger performance.
-Increase referral-based hiring to improve recruitment quality.
-Focus retention strategies on employees approaching 10 years tenure.
-Provide targeted training programs to improve KPI achievement rates.
